|:---:|:---:|:---|
| **o_e** | /əʊ/ | home, nose, rose, bone |
| **u_e** | /juː/ | cube, tube, cute, huge |
| **ar** | /ɑː/ | car, arm, star, park |
| **or** | /ɔː/ | fork, horse, corn, sport |
| **er** | /ə/ | tiger, sister, river, under |
| **ir** | /ɜː/ | bird, girl, shirt, skirt |
| **ur** | /ɜː/ | turtle, nurse, burger, purse |
